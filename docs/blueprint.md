# **App Name**: EduPath Navigator

## Core Features:

- Aptitude Quiz: A short quiz to assess student interests and skills.
- Stream Suggestion: Suggests suitable academic streams based on quiz results.
- Course to Career Mapping: Visually maps academic courses to potential career paths.
- Government College Directory: Directory of nearby government colleges, with filters for eligibility and cut-offs.
- College Details Display: Displays important college details, including courses offered, cut-off, and available facilities
- Timeline Tracker: Tracks key admission, scholarship, and exam deadlines, with user-configurable reminders.
- Personalized College Recommendations: Uses an AI tool incorporating student's profile data, and recommends matching available government colleges. Recommendations consider student location.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest Green (#34A05C) evoking growth, stability, and education.
- Background color: Pale Green (#E8F5E9), a very light tint of the primary color to provide a calm and accessible backdrop.
- Accent color: Golden Yellow (#FFC107) used sparingly to highlight important information and calls to action, symbolizing opportunity.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif with a modern look, is used throughout the app.
- Use clear, universally understandable icons related to education, careers, and notifications.
- Clean, intuitive layout optimized for both web and mobile. Prioritize ease of navigation for users in semi-urban and rural areas.
- Subtle animations to guide users through the quiz and course mapping, providing feedback and engagement without being distracting.
